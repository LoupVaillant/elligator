Known Elligator&nbsp;2 Implementations
======================================

- [Kleshni](https://github.com/Kleshni/Elligator-2) implements
  Elligator&nbsp;2 for Curve25519.
  However to our knowledge it does not implement the
  [special key exchange routines](key-exchange) needed to use the
  inverse map safely.
- [Libdecaf](https://sourceforge.net/projects/ed448goldilocks/)
  Implements Elligator&nbsp;2 for Curve25519 and Curve448.
- [Libsodium](https://libsodium.org) implements the Elligator&nbsp;2
  direct map for Curve25519 and [Ristretto255](https://ristretto.group).
- [Monocypher](https://monocypher.org) implements Elligator&nbsp;2 for
  Curve25519.

Please [contact us](mailto:elligator@loup-vaillant.fr) to have your
implementation added to this list.
